# SignalDesk — Product copy

Short, non-technical descriptions you can use on a landing page, in the app, or in pitch docs.

---

## One-liner

**SignalDesk turns the day’s news into clear, actionable intelligence for shareholders, investors, and business leaders—so you see not just what happened, but what it means for you.**

---

## How it works (3 short paragraphs)

**Always-on intelligence.**  
SignalDesk pulls in top business and general news every day and keeps your feed fresh. You can also hit “Fetch news now” anytime to load the latest headlines on demand.

**AI that explains, not just summarizes.**  
Each article is analyzed by AI to extract the real signal: a short takeaway, who’s involved, which topics and sectors are in play, and—most importantly—what it means for **shareholders**, **investors**, and **business leaders**. So you get plain-English implications and opportunities instead of raw headlines.

**Built for decision-makers.**  
No endless scrolling or guesswork. One feed, one place: summaries, implications, and opportunities so you can act faster and stay ahead.

---

## Bullets (for landing / feature list)

- **Daily refresh** — Your intelligence feed updates automatically every day; you can also refresh on demand with one click.
- **AI-powered implications** — Every story gets a clear takeaway plus what it means for shareholders, investors, and business.
- **One feed, zero noise** — Headlines, summaries, and actionable insights in one place.
- **For people who need to move fast** — Built for executives, investors, and operators who care what the news means, not just what it says.
